Principles followed in dental public health practices

 

   

  1. Public health work is done in areas where group responsibility is recognized .

  2. Public health work relies on team effort.

  3. Prevention of the diseases is the main goal.(prevention is better than cure).

  4. Deals with the research for the cause of the disease .

  5. Bio- statistical methods give a comparative analysis of the population seen and form basis for more studies.

  6. Public health workers deal with healthy people as well as the sick people.

  7. Health education & communication of the local population.

  8. Payment of health care made easy.
