Introduction: History & Definitions

  • Dental Implants: are permanent devices, surgically anchored in the oral cavity, often provide significant advantages over other fixed or removable prosthodontic options.

  • With more than 40 years history of dental implant,  the development of the ideal implant has been a major research subject in the field.
 
 
 

Implants Biomaterials

Dental Implants

  • Titanium(Ti), including Ti-6Al-4V alloy, is the first modern material used for dental implants, and it is still one of the most used in contemporary dental implants.

Dental Implants Biomaterials

  • Due to their suitable mechanical properties Ti & its Ti6Al4V alloys are the best materials for dental implants for load-bearing applications.

  • Different grades of titanium are used. Grade 5 (Ti-6Al-4V) titanium is 78% stronger than its next weaker Grade 4 cousin.
